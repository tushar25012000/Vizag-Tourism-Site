Discover Places

Popular Destinations

Vizag is home to many tourist spots may it be crystal-clear beaches to world class man-made structures to the finest foods, a place that has a lot to offer and will always leave you with a lasting impression.

  • Beach

    R.K.BEACH

    RK Beach is a famous sandy beach in Vizag with beautiful views.

  • Hills

    Kailasagiri

    Kailasagiri is a hilltop park in Vizag with gardens and a big Lord Shiva and Parvati statue.

  • Zoo Park

    Indira Gandhi Zoo Park

    Zoo Park is a sprawling zoo in Vizag housing various animals, birds, and reptiles.

  • Temple

    SIMHACHALAM TEMPLE

    Famous Hindu temple in Vizag known for its ancient architecture and spiritual significance.

  • Museum

    TU 142 Aircraft Museum

    A unique museum in Vizag, featuring a decommissioned aircraft displayed for visitors to explore.

  • Bridge

    Divis Bridge

    A picturesque walkway on the beach in Vizag, offering stunning views of the sea and coastline.
